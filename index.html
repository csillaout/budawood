ABOUT

Peter Kocsis

Education & Experiences

I spent the training of my school years in a great carpentry workshop, where I could learn everything about this profession. I am experienced in product manufacturing and on-site installation. That way, I can make everything from wood, from the roof to the floor. I like to create, so those works are closer to me where this can be fulfilled. For example: staircase, individual kitchen, floor, but I'm happy to put the furniture together to install the pre-made stairs, doors and windows. I have been in this profession for 25 years, so I have enough experience.
